Gon Mango Chocolate Bite

Milk chocolate shell with creamy mango filling. This is inspired by a cartoon named Gon the Dianosaur. The flavor is tropical, sweet and slightly tangy.

Ingredients

For the chocolate shell

  • 200g milk chocolate
  • Chocolate mold(silicon)

For the mango filling

  • 1/2 cup mango pulp
  • 2 tbsp condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp milk powder
  • 1 tbsp white chocolate

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate until smooth
  2. Pour chocolate into molds and coat the sides
  3. Refrigerate for 10 minutes to set the shell
  4. Cook mango pulp, condensed milk and butter on low heat
  5. Add milk powder and cook until the filling thickens
  6. Let the mango filling cool completely
  7. Add the filling into the chocolate shells
  8. Cover the meleted chocolate to seal
  9. Refrigerate for 15-20 minutes until set
  10. Remove the chocolate from the mold and serve>/li>
